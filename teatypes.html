Types Of Tea

We welcome you to use the menu to explore each of the five primary types of tea addressed on this website. You will see that each type has different flavor characteristics, different properties, and different prepartion instructions.

Tea first appeared in China approximately 4,000 to 5,000 years ago, and was generally grown in the southeastern region of China (e.g., south of the Yellow River). Tea has been used in China for medicinal purposes for thousands of years. According to legend, long ago an Emperor discovered that tea cured his upset stomach, leading to increased use and consumption of tea in China.

The bitterness of tea is typically correlated to the tea's caffeine level. The caffeine content of the tea typically increases with its bitterness. Interestingly, unlike other parts of the worlds, tea is not typically mixed with anything else such as milk or sugar before it is consumed in China.
