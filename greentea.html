Green Tea

Overview

Green tea is grown throughout China. Green tea is not fermented. Green tea contains caffeine. It has been know to have antioxidant properties.

Preparation

Take one teaspoon of green tea leaves. Now, take the tea leaves in a strainer/sieve and keep aside. Now, take a stainless steel pot/pan and boil the water. Now, place the sieve/ strainer over the cup or mug. Next, pour the hot water into the cup and let the tea steep for 3 minutes.
